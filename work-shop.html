Debriefs & Reporting

Where are we, where are we going?

Where We Are

  • Detailed post-program debriefs used to happen for PGME certificate programs and HMX.
  • Learnings from campaigns, recruitment, and creative aren't reliably feeding the next cycle.
  • Reporting is fragmented across program portfolios, the funnel, and creative — each group sees a different slice.
  • We have data — the gap is shared definitions, cadence, and what we actually act on together.

Discussion Prompts

  • What 3–5 metrics matter across our team — and which are unique to Program Marketing, AES, and Marketing Services?
  • What should debriefs look like moving forward — campaign, funnel, and creative in the same room — and who owns it?
  • How do we make reporting drive the next decision, not just describe the last cycle?
  • What should our reporting cadence look like a year from now?

Breakout

Design a reporting and debrief cadence — end to end

In small groups, pick one program or course you know well.

  1. Sketch a launch, check-in, reporting, and debrief cadence — who meets, when, and what gets reviewed.
  2. List the 3–5 metrics you'd report on, spanning campaign, funnel, and creative.
  3. Name the one decision each debrief should drive.

Be ready to walk the room through your cadence, metrics, and decisions.
